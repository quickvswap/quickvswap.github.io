QuickSwap leverages the efficiency of Polygon's Layer 2 technology, ensuring fast and low-cost transactions.
QuickSwap's architecture is based on the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM), making it compatible with Ethereum-based tokens. This compatibility allows users to seamlessly transfer their assets between the Ethereum and Polygon networks, benefiting from the scalability and low fees of Polygon while staying connected to the broader Ethereum ecosystem.
The protocol's native token, QUICK, plays a central role in the QuickSwap ecosystem. It serves as a governance token, allowing holders to participate in the decision-making process and shape the future development of the platform. Additionally, QUICK holders can stake their tokens in liquidity pools to earn attractive yields and participate in the platform's governance rewards.
